# Challenges for Fast Learners!

Pick any challenge below and build it using `template.py` as your starting point.
There are no rules — experiment, break things, and have fun!

---

## Beginner Challenges

1. **Rainbow Bouncer** — Make the bouncing ball change color every time it hits a wall.
2. **Two Players** — Add a second paddle/player controlled with WASD keys.
3. **Score Counter** — Add a score that goes up every 5 seconds.
4. **Starfield** — Display 200 random white dots on a black background and make them slowly drift downward.
5. **Color Mixer** — Draw three overlapping transparent circles (red, green, blue) to show color mixing.

---

## Intermediate Challenges

6. **Pong** — Two paddles, one ball, first to 5 points wins.
7. **Snake** — Classic snake game: eat food, grow longer, don't hit yourself.
8. **Shooting Game** — Move left/right, press Space to shoot upward, destroy falling enemies.
9. **Fireworks** — Click anywhere on the screen to launch a firework that explodes into particles.
10. **Clock** — Draw a working analogue clock using `math.sin` and `math.cos`.

---

## Hard Challenges

11. **Platformer** — A character that can jump and land on platforms.
12. **Maze** — Generate a random maze and let the player walk through it.
13. **Card Memory Game** — Flip cards to find matching pairs.
14. **Particle System** — Emit hundreds of coloured particles from the mouse position with gravity.
15. **Your Own Game** — Invent something completely new!

---

> Tip: Start small, get one thing working, then add the next feature.
